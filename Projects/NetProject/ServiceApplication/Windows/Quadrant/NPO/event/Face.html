Join us for a spooktacular Halloween event for all ages!
Arrive in costume for an evening of trick-or-treating at participating merchants marked with balloons on their door, a Halloween photo-booth, face painting, and jumping in the Springfree trampoline.

~ Trick or Treating at participating RTC Merchants, 4-7 pm (free while supplies last)
~ Costume photos in the Halloween Photobooth, 4-7 pm (free)
~ Springfree trampoline jumping, 4-7 pm (free)
~ Merry Makers face painting, 4-8 pm (free)
~ Enjoy Halloween specials at RTC Merchants, mall hours
